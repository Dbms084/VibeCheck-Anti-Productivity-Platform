## What I Built
**VibeCheck™** is the ultimate Anti-Productivity Platform designed specifically to stop you from grinding. 

In a world obsessed with optimizing every second for maximum output, VibeCheck takes the opposite approach. It is a satirical dashboard built with a "chaotic cottagecore" aesthetic that actively discourages you from doing any actual work. Instead of tracking tasks, it tracks your vibes. 

Key features include:
- **Infinity Pomodoro**: A timer that rewards you the longer you *don't* work. 
- **The Guilt-Free Generator**: A sassy, anti-logic life coach that gives you terrible, unhelpful advice on why you should just go buy an iced latte instead of writing code.
- **Overtime Garden**: A "Forest-style" app feature that grows flowers on your screen every 15 minutes you work overtime—nature's way of claiming back your wasted time!
- **NerdGate**: A strict gatekeeper that prevents anyone with too much logic from entering.

## How I Built It
VibeCheck was built with a modern stack, used exclusively for the most unserious purposes:
- **Next.js 16 (App Router) & React 19**: To ensure our lack of productivity is blazingly fast.
- **Tailwind CSS v4 & Framer Motion**: Powering the unapologetically chaotic, smooth, and aggressively aesthetic cottagecore animations.
- **Google Generative AI (`gemini-1.5-flash`)**: The brains behind the operation. We hooked up the Gemini API to the Guilt-Free Generator to process user guilt and spit out 1-2 sentence savage replies telling them to go touch grass.
